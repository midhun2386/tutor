# Beginner Lesson Plan: Foundational English

## Learning Objective
Students will be able to identify common household items and use basic greetings in English.

## Key Concepts
- Basic Greetings (Hello, Hi, Good morning)
- Household Vocabulary (Chair, Table, Bed, Door)
- Simple Pronouns (I, You, This, That)

## Lesson Content
1. **Greetings**:
   - "Hello, how are you?"
   - "I am fine, thank you."
2. **The Home**:
   - "This is a chair."
   - "That is a door."
3. **Common Verbs**:
   - Sit, Stand, Open, Close.

## Practice Exercise
- Identify three items in your room and say their names in English.
- Greet a family member using a new phrase.
