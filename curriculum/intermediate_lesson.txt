# Intermediate Lesson Plan: Sentence Formation & Daily Routines

## Learning Objective
Students will be able to construct complete sentences describing their daily routines and make simple requests in a home setting.

## Key Concepts
- Present Continuous Tense (I am cooking, She is reading)
- Helping Verbs (Can, May, Will)
- Prepositions of Place (In, On, Under, Beside)

## Lesson Content
1. **Daily Routines**:
   - "I am cooking dinner in the kitchen."
   - "He is sleeping on the bed."
2. **Making Requests**:
   - "Can you please open the window?"
   - "May I sit here?"
3. **Compound Sentences**:
   - "I like tea, but I prefer coffee."

## Practice Exercise
- Describe what three people in your house are doing right now.
- Ask someone for help with a household chore using "Can you...".
